FAQ

Common questions

What counts as a "compliance module"?

Each module covers one NZ legislation area — Health & Safety (HSWA), Privacy Act, AML/CFT, Employment Relations, Environmental (RMA), Building Act, Consumer Protection, or Body Corporate. You choose which modules are relevant to your business. On Starter you get 2; on Professional you get 5.

What's included in the free trial?

Full access to your chosen plan for 14 days — no credit card required. You can set up your compliance modules, run your first AI analysis, and see your compliance score before committing. At the end of the trial, you choose a plan or your account is paused (data retained for 30 days).

Is this a substitute for legal advice?

No — Modular Compliance is a compliance management tool, not a law firm. It helps you monitor, track, and manage your obligations, but it doesn't provide legal advice. For complex legal questions, consult a qualified NZ lawyer. That said, many businesses use MC to reduce how often they need to.

Where is my data stored?

The Modular Compliance application runs on Railway's US-West region infrastructure. Your compliance data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We disclose offshore data storage clearly in our Privacy Policy as required by IPP 12 of the NZ Privacy Act 2020. Cloudflare CDN is used for this marketing website.

Can I change plans or cancel anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from within your account settings. There are no lock-in contracts. If you cancel, you retain access until the end of your current billing period. Audit trail data is retained for the legislatively required 7-year period.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or charities?

Yes — NZ registered charities and non-profits are eligible for a 30% discount on Starter and Professional plans. Contact us at info@instilligent.com with your Charities Register number to apply.
