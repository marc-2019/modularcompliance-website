What it does

A structured record of your compliance work

Legislation is mapped into frameworks, frameworks into controls, controls into tasks. Your team works the tasks; the system keeps the auditable record.

Compliance register

Legislation mapped into structured frameworks, controls and tasks — populated from AI-generated and partner-supplied templates that you review and adapt as your starting point.

Completion records

Records who completed what and when, and captures policy and framework terms acceptance with an audit trail.

Task dashboard

The dashboard shows overdue tasks from your register so the work that needs attention is visible — a view of your records, not automated monitoring.

Evidence & documents

Stores the supporting evidence and documents behind your compliance record, alongside the tasks they relate to.

Incident register

Record incidents with severity levels as part of your compliance record. The register is your internal record — it does not notify or file with any regulator.

Platform foundations

Role-based access control, SSO (SAML 2.0 & OIDC — Enterprise tier), REST API access (Professional tier and above), and configurable task workflows — manually configured task chains, not event-driven automation.

Modules

Framework template modules

Modular Compliance ships seeded framework templates for NZ Privacy Act 2020, NZ Health and Safety at Work Act 2015, NZ Employment Relations Act 2000 and ISO 9001 Quality Management, plus operational templates for Body Corporate, Maritime, Food Safety and Environmental compliance. You can also build custom frameworks. Each module is a template library of obligations, controls and tasks — a starting point your team reviews and adapts, not automated monitoring.

Honest boundaries

What it doesn't do

Modular Compliance does not connect to your operational systems, monitor transactions, screen activity, or take regulatory action on your behalf. Templates are AI-generated and partner-supplied starting points — you remain responsible for their accuracy and applicability to your business. The compliance work is done by your people; Modular Compliance keeps the record.
